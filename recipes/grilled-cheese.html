Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  2. Lay one slice, butter side down, in heated skillet
  3. Top with cheese and other slice of bread, butter side up
  4. Cook until golden brown, flip and cook til done
